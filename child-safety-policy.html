⚠️ Important Notice: Match Master is an adult dating application exclusively for users aged 18 years and older. The use of this application by minors is strictly prohibited.

1. Age Restrictions

Match Master enforces strict age verification and restriction policies:

  • All users must be at least 18 years of age to create an account
  • Users are required to confirm their age during registration
  • We reserve the right to verify user age and request identification
  • Any account found to belong to a minor will be immediately terminated

2. Protection Measures

We implement multiple safety mechanisms to protect all users:

  • User Reporting: In-app reporting system for inappropriate behavior or content
  • Content Moderation: Automated and manual review of user profiles and content
  • Blocking Features: Users can block and report other users at any time
  • Account Suspension: Immediate action taken against violators of our policies

3. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following content and behavior are strictly prohibited:

  • Sexual exploitation or abuse of minors in any form

  • Grooming, solicitation, or inappropriate contact with minors
  • Impersonation or misrepresentation of age
  • Any content that sexualizes or endangers children

4. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report safety concerns through multiple channels:

  • In-App Reporting: Report button available on all user profiles and conversations

  • Response Time: All reports are reviewed within 24 hours

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Match Master is committed to:

  • Complying with all applicable laws regarding child safety
  • Cooperating fully with law enforcement agencies
  • Reporting suspected child abuse to relevant authorities (including NCMEC in the United States)
  • Preserving evidence for legal proceedings when required

6. Privacy and Data Protection

We protect user data while maintaining safety:

  • User reports are handled confidentially
  • Personal information is shared with authorities only when legally required
  • Data retention follows applicable legal requirements

7. Community Guidelines

All users must:

  • Treat other users with respect
  • Be truthful about their age and identity
  • Report suspicious or inappropriate behavior immediately

Material changes will be communicated to users through the application.

10. Zero Tolerance

Match Master maintains a zero-tolerance policy for any activity that endangers children. Violations will result in:
