How to Delete Your Account and Data

Method 1: Delete Account Through the App

  1. Open the Match Master app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down to "Account Management"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion by entering your password
  6. Tap "Permanently Delete Account"

⚠️ This action is PERMANENT and CANNOT be undone after the recovery period.

Method 2: Request Data Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

  • General Support: support@matchmaster.app
  • Data Protection (EU): data-protection@matchmaster.app

Please include in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your account username
  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information (name, bio, photos)
  • Match history and preferences
  • Messages and conversations
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics
  • Account credentials

Note: Backups may persist for up to 90 days for technical reasons.

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and safety reasons, we may retain:

  • Transaction records (for legal compliance)
  • Reports of violations or abuse
  • Data required by law enforcement
  • Anonymized analytics data (no personal identifiers)

This retained data cannot be linked back to you.

Account Recovery Period

  • You have 30 days to recover your deleted account
  • After 30 days, deletion is permanent
  • To recover: Log in within 30 days and confirm restoration

Data Export Before Deletion

  1. Go to Profile → Settings → Privacy
  2. Tap "Download My Data"
  3. Wait for an email with a download link
  4. The download link expires in 7 days

You will receive:

  • Profile information (JSON format)
  • Photos (ZIP archive)
  • Match history
  • Message history

Third-Party Login Accounts

If you signed in with Google or Facebook:

  • Deleting your Match Master account does NOT delete your Google or Facebook account
  • You should also revoke app permissions:
    • Google: myaccount.google.com/permissions
    • Facebook: facebook.com/settings → Apps and Websites

Contact Information

General: support@matchmaster.app
EU Users (GDPR): data-protection@matchmaster.app
Legal: legal@matchmaster.app

Response time: Within 30 days

⚠️
Important: Account deletion is permanent after 30 days. Make sure to export your data before deleting if you want to keep a copy.
