MatchMaster Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

About this project: MatchMaster is an independent software project developed and operated by an individual developer. At this stage, MatchMaster is provided on a beta and experimental basis to collect feedback and improve the product.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the MatchMaster app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, date of birth, gender, email address, phone number, profile photos
  • Location Data: GPS location for matchmaking within your area (if enabled by you)
  • User Content: Photos, preferences, interests, and bio information you provide
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, matches, messages, and feature usage

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide matchmaking and dating services
  • Show your profile to potential matches (based on your settings)
  • Enable communication between matched users
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send notifications about matches and messages
  • Prevent fraud and help ensure platform safety
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We may share your data in the following ways:

  • With Other Users: Your profile is visible to other users according to your privacy settings and match preferences
  • Service Providers: Technical service providers (for example, Firebase and Cloudinary) to operate and improve the service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: If the project is transferred (e.g., acquisition), your data may be transferred as part of that process

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage (e.g., Firebase)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Ongoing improvements to security practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights (GDPR-style Rights)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data (if available)
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact: matchmastersergey@gmail.com

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Until you delete your account
  • Deleted accounts: Up to 30 days for recovery, then permanently deleted
  • Backups: May persist in backups for up to 90 days for technical reasons
  • Legal holds: Retained as required by law

7. Age Restrictions

MatchMaster is only for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a minor has created an account, it will be deleted.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We may use:

  • Essential technologies needed for app functionality
  • Analytics to understand app usage
  • Performance monitoring tools

You can manage privacy settings in your device settings where available.

9. Third-Party Services

We may integrate with third-party services, such as:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Database, Analytics)
  • Google Sign-In and Facebook Login (if enabled)
  • Cloudinary (Image hosting)
  • Location Services

Each service has its own privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted with an updated “Last Updated” date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
