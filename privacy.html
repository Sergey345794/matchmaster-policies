Match Master Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, date of birth, gender, email address, phone number, profile photos
  • Location Data: GPS location for matchmaking within your area
  • User Content: Photos, preferences, interests, and bio information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, matches, messages, and feature usage

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide matchmaking and dating services
  • Show your profile to potential matches
  • Enable communication between matched users
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send notifications about matches and messages
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform safety
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We share your data in the following ways:

  • With Other Users: Your profile is visible to other users based on match preferences
  • Service Providers: Firebase, Cloudinary, and other technical service providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We DO NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage with Firebase
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@matchmaster.app

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Until you delete your account
  • Deleted accounts: Up to 30 days for recovery, then permanently deleted
  • Backups: May persist in backups for up to 90 days
  • Legal holds: Retained as required by law

7. Age Restrictions

Match Master is only for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a minor has created an account, it will be immediately deleted.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use:

  • Essential cookies for app functionality
  • Analytics to understand app usage
  • Performance monitoring tools

You can manage cookie preferences in your device settings.

9. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Database, Analytics)
  • Google Sign-In & Facebook Login
  • Cloudinary (Image hosting)
  • Location Services

Each service has its own privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
